About.

Welcome to the BBQ Life

We're all about talking about what makes BBQ so great – the fire, the smoky flavors, and the traditions that come with it. We’re here to cheer on the art of barbecue, no matter how you do it. If you're just starting out or you’re a pro, we’re here to help you get better and love BBQ even more.

BBQ is more than just cooking. It’s an event. It’s about being with food, nature, and the people around you. Grilling brings people together, and that’s what we want to do: create a worldwide group of folks who love cooking outdoors.

🧱 Where We Started

Some friends who loved to grill got together, and now it’s turned into a big spot for all things BBQ. We saw that there were a bunch of places to buy stuff, but not many that got what it takes to be a real BBQ fan.

So, we made this place where anyone can learn and share – newbies and experts alike. It’s cool if you mess up your first steak, as long as you try harder the next time. BBQ is a way of life here, not just something you do on weekends.

🌎 What We Want to Do

We want to help people everywhere cook, eat, and live better by getting outside and grilling.

We want to teach you how to grill and smoke, look at BBQs from all over, and share what we know about grills, fuel, how to keep them up, and how to stay safe. Plus, we’ve got recipes for meats, veggies, and more.

We also want to encourage cooking that’s good for the planet and create a friendly BBQ group where everyone’s welcome.

 No secrets. Just good grilling.

We think it's about real food, fire, and people. We want to give you real help based on our own BBQ time.

We like old ways and new ideas, and we’re always learning because that’s what BBQ is all about: trying new stuff and loving the flame.
